Spin to Win ‍

Required $FKS tokens to play Spin!

1. Add the Metamask browser extension on your PC or Laptop.

2. Add the Bsc Mainnet to your metamask: https://chainlist.org/chain/56

3. You can buy any amount of $FKS tokens from Pancakeswap to play spin

***You will require BNB to buy $FKS tokens, you can get BNB from centralized exchange like Binance***

4. Make sure your Metamask is on the BSC Binance Smart Chain in step 2.

5. Go to spin.flokispin.com click play spin.

FAQs

How does it works?

A player place a preferred amount of their $FKS tokens to play spin, which can result in doubling(2x) of the amount depending on the outcome of the spin

How does the outcome works?

The spin result is multiplied by the amount of $FKS tokens a player uses to play. Then the reward is distributed to the player

Is this the full version of the game?

Yes, but the team are still working on other upgrades to improve the game

How many $FKS tokens do I required to play spin?

You can play spin with any amount of $FKS tokens

How many times can I spin?‍

Spin is unlimited as far as you have $FKS tokens to play spin

How do I get my reward if I win?

Spin rewards are distributed to winners every 24hrs by the spin admin

Huge Burn mechanism

The spin is designed to acquire $FKS tokens used to play spin, where 50% of the daily spin tokens accrued in the daily spin vault is burnt, and the other 50% is transferrd to the spin vault for reward and long sustainability.

The burn is done every 24hrs
